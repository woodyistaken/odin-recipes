Lasagna

Description

A type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made in a very wide, flat sheets. It is made of stacked players of pasta alternating with fillings. The dish may be topped with grated cheese, which melts during baking. Baked pasta is cut into single-serving square or rectangular portions.

Ingredients

  • Cheese Filling
  • Meat
  • Sauce
  • Spinach

Steps

  1. Boil Pasta
  2. Prepare meat sauce
  3. Combine cheese mixture
  4. Layer and Bake
